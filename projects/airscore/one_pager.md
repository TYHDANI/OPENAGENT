# AirScore — Smart Home Air Intelligence

## Problem
Homeowners and property managers have no easy way to monitor indoor air quality across rooms and properties. Poor IAQ causes health issues, yet existing solutions are fragmented — one app per sensor brand, no fleet view for commercial properties.

## Solution
AirScore combines B2C home air quality monitoring with B2B fleet HVAC management. Track PM2.5, CO2, VOC, humidity, and temperature room-by-room with EPA-compliant AQI scoring and smart alerts.

## Core Features
1. **Dashboard** — Overall AQI score with room-by-room breakdown, alert banners, filter maintenance reminders
2. **Room Detail** — 6 pollutant metrics per room with color-coded status indicators (good/moderate/bad)
3. **Trends** — 24h/7d/30d trend charts for AQI, PM2.5, CO2, and humidity with min/max/avg stats
4. **Fleet Mode (B2B)** — Multi-property dashboard with zone-level AQI monitoring, maintenance scheduling

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 1 property, 3 rooms, basic AQI
- **Pro ($9.99/mo)**: 3 properties, 15 rooms, trends, alerts, export
- **Fleet ($29.99/mo)**: 50 properties, 500 rooms, fleet dashboard, reporting

## Target Market
- Health-conscious homeowners (60M US homes with smart devices)
- Commercial property managers (2M US commercial buildings)
- TAM: $4.2B indoor air quality market growing 8% CAGR

## Competitive Edge
- Dual B2C/B2B in one app (competitors are one or the other)
- EPA-compliant AQI calculation (not proprietary scores)
- Filter lifecycle tracking reduces HVAC costs
