# Griffin — Digital Erasure & Safe Relocation Platform

## Elevator Pitch
"The Invisible Company." A vertically integrated platform for people who need to disappear safely — combining automated digital erasure (SaaS), concierge case management, and physical relocation coordination. Think Witness Protection for civilians, built on a privacy-first tech stack.

## The Problem
People fleeing domestic abuse, stalking, extreme debt, cult situations, or toxic corporate dynamics have to cobble together help from lawyers, shelters, IT specialists, and moving companies — none of whom talk to each other. Fragmented services exist (DeleteMe for data brokers at ~$130/yr, underfunded DV relocation orgs, executive protection firms at $$$) but NOTHING combines all layers into one platform.

## Core App Concept

### Layer 1: Digital Erasure (SaaS — iOS App)
- Automated account deletion across 200+ platforms
- Email purging and alias replacement ("new identity inbox")
- Social media scrubbing + GDPR/CCPA deletion request management
- Data broker opt-out automation (Spokeo, WhitePages, BeenVerified, etc.)
- Device wipe guidance and new device provisioning checklist
- VPN + privacy stack setup wizard
- Digital footprint audit score

### Layer 2: Concierge Coordination (Managed Service)
- Dedicated case manager assigned through app
- Secure encrypted messaging (Signal-level E2E encryption)
- New address, PO box, and mail forwarding setup guidance
- Credit freeze and financial separation planning
- Legal referral network (restraining orders, name changes)
- Financial continuity planning (bank transitions, employer references, credit rebuilding)

### Layer 3: Physical Relocation (Premium Tier)
- Vetted escort coordination (ex-law enforcement, military, private security)
- Counter-surveillance sweep checklist (AirTag detection, stalkerware scan, social graph analysis)
- Safe house network directory
- Destination city setup (housing leads, employment connections)
- 24-72 hour execution window planning

## The "Sanctuary Protocol" — Intake/Vetting Flow
Critical differentiator. High-friction, high-touch gateway to ensure 100% legitimacy:
1. **Phase 0**: Anonymous encrypted contact (PGP-encrypted intake form, no identifying info needed)
2. **Phase 1**: Confidential crisis triage (master's-level social worker, threat categorization)
3. **Phase 2**: Verification crucible (investigator/paralegal under attorney supervision — verify victim, not perpetrator)
4. **Phase 3**: Plan building (case manager, data compartmentalization, "Go Signal" only when ready)
5. **Phase 4**: Execution (digital + physical in parallel, followed by "Ghost Protocol" ongoing monitoring)

## Competitive Landscape
- **DeleteMe** (~$130/yr) — Data broker removal ONLY, no full erasure, no relocation
- **Privacy.com / MySudo** — Financial/identity masking only
- **DV relocation orgs** — Underfunded, no tech layer, fragmented
- **Executive protection firms** — $$$, not consumer-facing
- **NO platform combines digital + physical + legal in one product**

## The MOAT
1. **Network of vetted operators** — Ex-FBI, retired marshals, private security. Credentialed franchise model with strict onboarding, liability insurance, quarterly audits. This is the real IP
2. **Trust brand** — "Going Griffin" becomes slang for disappearing safely. Brand = everything in this market
3. **Legal framework** — Attorney-client privilege wrapper, three-part sign-off process (social worker + investigator + staff attorney)
4. **Operational security expertise** — Counter-surveillance protocols, canary trap documents, data compartmentalization

## Technical Stack (iOS)
- SwiftUI for clean, calming UI (trauma-informed design — no aggressive colors, gentle animations)
- CryptoKit for E2E encryption
- Core Bluetooth for AirTag/tracker detection
- Local-only data storage option (no cloud requirement for sensitive info)
- Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID mandatory)
- StoreKit 2 for subscriptions
- Push notifications for deletion progress and broker monitoring alerts
- Background tasks for ongoing data broker scanning

## Design Philosophy
- **Trauma-informed UI**: Calm, minimal, no red/aggressive colors. Gentle onboarding. "You're safe here" energy
- **Privacy-first**: Default to local storage. Cloud sync opt-in only. No analytics, no tracking pixels
- **Anonymity branding**: App icon is deliberately nondescript. Can be hidden in folder. No notifications that reveal app purpose
- **Logo**: Griffin silhouette that dissolves/becomes transparent at edges. Monochrome. No color needed
- **Tagline**: "Seen by no one. Protected by us."

## B2B Revenue Layer
- **Referral partnerships**: DV shelters, family law attorneys, HR departments, trauma therapists
- **Insurance play** (long-term): "Disappearance coverage" rider on homeowner's/umbrella insurance
- **Corporate contracts**: Whistleblower protection programs for Fortune 500 companies

## Risk Assessment
- **Vetting**: Must ensure helping victims, not criminals. Three-part sign-off mitigates this
- **Liability**: Escorts and physical relocation need serious insurance and legal structure
- **Trust**: Entire brand lives and dies on discretion. Insider threat is biggest risk (canary traps, security clearance vetting, NDA with liquidated damages)
- **Children**: Biggest legal landmine. Dedicated family law attorney on retainer in every state needed
- **Financial continuity**: Where does the paycheck go? Bank transition planning is critical

"Going Griffin" as cultural slang. Domain: griffin.co or griffinprotocol.com. Internal language: clients = "Invisibles", new life = "The Other Side", execution day = "The Fade."
