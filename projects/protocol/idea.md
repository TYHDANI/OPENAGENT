# Protocol — AI Longevity & Health Protocol Engine

## Problem
People want to optimize their health like Bryan Johnson but have no way to track biomarkers, get personalized protocols, or manage dopamine levels without expensive clinics ($10K+/year). 500M+ Apple Watch users generate health data daily but get zero actionable protocols from it.

## Solution
The Bryan Johnson experience for everyone. AI-powered health optimization that ingests Apple Health + Watch data and generates personalized daily protocols.
- **Biomarker Dashboard**: Track 50+ health metrics across 6 categories (sleep architecture, cardiovascular, metabolic, hormonal, body composition, cognitive) with trend analysis and biological age scoring
- **Protocol Engine**: Huberman-style zero-cost intervention library (cold exposure timing, light protocols, breathing techniques, exercise sequencing, supplement stacks) personalized to YOUR data
- **Dopamine Management System**: Track stimulus intake (caffeine, social media, sugar, alcohol) and predict crash windows using Huberman's dopamine wave pool model. Suggest recovery protocols
- **Sleep Architecture Analyzer**: Deep sleep, REM, HRV overnight trends. Bryan Johnson's nighttime erection equivalent — aggregate health scoring that can't be gamed
- **Weekly Longevity Score**: AI-generated biological age estimate with actionable breakdown of what's helping and hurting
- **Protocol Library**: 200+ evidence-based protocols from peer-reviewed research, categorized by goal (energy, sleep, focus, recovery, longevity)

## Target Audience
Health-conscious adults 25-55 who own Apple Watch/iPhone. Biohackers, fitness enthusiasts, longevity-curious professionals. People who follow Huberman, Bryan Johnson, Peter Attia, Rhonda Patrick. 200M+ potential users globally.

## Monetization
Freemium with subscription:
- Free: Basic tracking, 3 protocols/week, sleep score
- Analyst ($12.99/mo or $99.99/yr): Full protocol engine, dopamine tracking, 50+ biomarkers, weekly longevity score
- Pro ($24.99/mo or $199.99/yr): Biological age tracking, advanced biomarker correlations, supplement interaction checker, AI health advisor chat, data export

## Competition
- **Apple Health**: Free but zero protocols or actionable insights
- **Whoop** ($30/mo): Recovery/strain only, no protocols, no longevity
- **Oura** ($5.99/mo): Sleep + readiness only, no protocols
- **InsideTracker** ($249-999/test): Blood-test based, expensive, no daily protocols
- **None** combine Apple Health data + personalized protocols + dopamine management + longevity scoring
