# ScaleForge — AI Solopreneur Operating System

## Problem
Solo entrepreneurs waste 60%+ of their time on repetitive tasks (content creation, trend monitoring, bookkeeping, lead generation) instead of building. An 18-year-old proved you can run an entire company with 15 AI agents for $400/month — but setting that up requires technical knowledge most entrepreneurs don't have.

## Solution
A mobile-first AI operating system that gives every solopreneur a virtual team of AI employees.
- **AI Department System**: Pre-built AI agents for Content (trend detection + draft generation + scheduling), Sales (lead scoring, follow-up sequences, CRM), Operations (task management, competitor monitoring, KPI tracking), Finance (revenue tracking, expense categorization, tax prep estimates)
- **MOAT Scoring Engine**: Alex Hormozi's private equity framework built in — input any business idea and get a 1-40 score across Margin, Operations, Advantage, TAM with specific go/fix/flee recommendations
- **Content Pipeline**: Inspired by OpenClaw model — Trend agent finds viral topics → Writer agent creates platform-specific drafts → User approves → Auto-schedules across platforms
- **Leverage Calculator**: Naval Ravikant's leverage framework — shows what % of your time is leveraged (code, media, capital) vs traded (hourly work). Recommendations to increase leverage
- **Entrepreneur Lifecycle Tracker**: Hormozi's 6 stages — shows where you are and what to focus on to break through to the next stage
- **Revenue Dashboard**: Connect Stripe/Gumroad/App Store Connect to track MRR, churn, LTV, CAC in real-time

## Target Audience
Solopreneurs, freelancers, indie hackers, small business owners (1-5 employees) who want AI to handle operational overhead. 50M+ solopreneurs in US alone. Age 22-45, tech-forward, budget-conscious.

## Monetization
Freemium with subscription:
- Free: 1 AI agent, basic MOAT scoring, manual task management
- Pro ($19.99/mo or $149.99/yr): 5 AI agents, full MOAT framework, content pipeline, leverage calculator, revenue dashboard
- Scale ($49.99/mo or $399.99/yr): Unlimited agents, API integrations (Stripe, social platforms), team collaboration, custom agent training
- B2B: $29/seat/mo for agencies and small teams

## Competition
- **Notion AI** ($10/mo): Note-taking with AI, no business frameworks or agent system
- **Jasper** ($49/mo): Content only, no business intelligence
- **Copy.ai** ($36/mo): Content only, no operations or finance
- **Monday.com** ($8/seat): Project management, no AI agents, no business frameworks
- **None** combine AI agents + MOAT scoring + content pipeline + leverage tracking

## Notes
Inspired by Vim's OpenClaw setup (15 AI agents, $400/month, zero coding), Alex Hormozi's MOAT framework, Naval Ravikant's leverage and specific knowledge concepts, and Chris Kerner's zero-employee business model. SwiftUI + CloudKit for data sync. On-device AI for drafts, API calls for advanced agents. StoreKit 2 subscriptions.
