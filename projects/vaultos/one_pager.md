# VaultOS — Crypto Wealth Intelligence

## Problem
Crypto investors with multi-entity structures (trusts, LLCs, IRAs) have no unified tool for risk assessment, tax optimization, and succession planning. They juggle 5+ apps and spreadsheets, missing wash sales across entities and leaving money on the table.

## Solution
VaultOS combines pre-investment risk scanning, active tax management, and estate succession planning into one premium iOS app built for serious crypto wealth.

## Core Features
1. **Sentinel Risk Scanner** — 15-factor weighted scoring for DeFi protocols (TVL trend, collateral ratio, APY volatility, audit recency, etc.)
2. **Multi-Entity Portfolio** — Track holdings across Individual, Trust, LLC, S-Corp, and IRA accounts with Coinbase/Kraken/Gemini/iTrustCapital integration
3. **Tax Center** — FIFO/LIFO/HIFO cost basis, cross-entity wash sale detection, quarterly estimated tax calculations with federal rates
4. **Legacy Planning** — Succession plans with dead man's switch, beneficiary allocation validation, inactivity triggers

## Revenue Model
- **Free**: 1 entity, basic risk scanning
- **Pro ($14.99/mo)**: 5 entities, wash sale detection, Form 8949, succession planning
- **Family ($24.99/mo)**: 10 entities, 25 beneficiaries, all features

## Target Market
- Crypto investors with $10K+ portfolios
