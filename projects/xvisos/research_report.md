## Pain Point Validation

**Problem:** iPhone camera becomes unusable below 20 lux (poor natural light).

**Current Solutions & Barriers:**
- FLIR One Gen 3 ($500+): Hardware barrier, separate device
- Seek Thermal ($200-300): Hardware investment, limited compatibility
- Night Vision Camera app (free): Poor effects, unreliable 30fps
- iOS Night Mode (free): No thermal capability

**XvisOS Gap:** Software-only, zero cost, 4 vision modes, sustained 30fps
